What is a wallet?

Manage your NFTs

How can I manage my NFTs at all?

AlphaNotary offers additional functionalities to bring a complete service, including the removal function

NFTs are meant to stay immutable through the time, but if you want to remove some of them, you can! Take care that removing your files will not bring any advantage, nor to you, neither to AlphaNotary or the Blockchain, because everything is only saved in the Blockchain, where blocks are used only once because it is a one-way chain where new blocks are added to the end. To understand better the Blockchain structure, or to test this function, you can run the application in local using Truffle suite.
